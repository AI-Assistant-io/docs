# Release v1.8.0

**Release Date:** 2026-01-23

---

## New Features

### New Templates: WeWantYourCar and ServiceLapsed (PR #21, Issue #20)
Replaced the "NewArrival" template with two new targeted outreach templates.

#### WeWantYourCar
Outreach to customers about purchasing their pre-owned vehicle.

- Explains high demand for their vehicle make at Dick Lovett
- Offers straightforward, secure, price-advantageous sale process
- Collection service available
- Handles part-exchange enquiries
- Updates records if customer no longer owns the vehicle

**Example opening:**
> "Dear Mr Kelk, It has been incredibly busy at Dick Lovett Bristol and the desire to own a BMW continues to stimulate high demand..."

#### ServiceLapsed
Re-engagement outreach for customers who haven't visited for a while.

- Offers complimentary Vehicle Health Check (FREE)
- Includes mini valet (FREE)
- Covers brakes, lights, tyres, and auxiliary belts
- Warm, welcoming tone
- Option to book or request service interval information

**Example opening:**
> "Dear Mr Kelk, It's lovingly designed and engineered, and one of the most beautiful cars on the road, but your BMW needs regular attention..."

## Technical Changes

- Removed "NewArrival" template
- Added "WeWantYourCar" template with message and context
- Added "ServiceLapsed" template with message and context
- Added new fields to `DEMO_DATA["Customer"]`: title, lastName, vehicleMake, regNo
- Added `branchCode` to `DEMO_DATA["Branch"]`
- Updated `get_demo_data()` to include Customer fields for all templates
