# Release v1.5.0

**Release Date:** 2026-01-23

---

## New Features

### Expanded Automotive Knowledge (PR #15, Issue #13)
Enhanced the main agent (Zoe) with comprehensive automotive terminology and domain knowledge to better assist customers.

- **Service Types:** MOT, Annual, Interim, Major, and Recall services explained
- **Parts & Repairs:** Common components (brakes, tyres, suspension, clutch, battery, timing belt, DPF, EGR, A/C)
- **Warning Lights:** Guidance on red vs amber lights and recommended actions
- **Fuel Types:** Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, PHEV, BEV, and MHEV explanations
- **Emissions Compliance:** ULEZ, CAZ, and Euro 6 information
- **Finance Terms:** PCP, HP, balloon payment, settlement figure, equity, part-exchange, GAP insurance
- **Customer Phrases:** Common informal expressions and how to respond appropriately

## Improvements
